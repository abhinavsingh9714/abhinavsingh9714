# Research Associate — Management Development Institute, Gurgaon (Jan 2024 – Jul 2024)

**Two parallel research tracks:**

## 1. AI-Driven CRM Research (Published Book)

Built ML models supporting a published book on AI-driven Customer Relationship Management:
- **Recommendation systems** — collaborative filtering and content-based models for customer personalization
- **Customer churn prediction** — XGBoost and scikit-learn ensemble models on structured tabular CRM data; demonstrated measurable improvement in retention targeting
- **Dynamic pricing strategies** — ML-based pricing models for revenue optimization
- Practical insights on marketing automation, sales funnel optimization, and customer service AI

## 2. AI Governance Research for India's NSCS

Collaborated with India's National Security Council Secretariat (NSCS) to research AI governance frameworks:
- Analyzed 50+ cybersecurity threats relevant to AI systems
- Compared governance frameworks across four major jurisdictions: EU AI Act, UK AI Safety Institute approach, USA NIST AI Risk Management Framework, Australia AI Ethics Principles
- Directly contributed to India's AI-driven cybersecurity policy thinking
- Assessed regulatory approaches: risk-based (EU) vs. sector-specific (UK) vs. voluntary frameworks (USA/Australia)
